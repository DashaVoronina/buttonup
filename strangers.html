Hello stranger!

We are Anna and Arina - the founders of the STRANGERS brand.

What do you pay attention to when you meet someone?

We, like thousands of our peers who are in love with style, meet strangers by their clothes. She can tell more about a person than words: look at me - here I am.

STRANGERS are clothes for those who are not afraid to switch to "you" already at the first meeting. Do you prefer sport chic for walking around the city or do you like the aesthetics of laconic minimalism?

In any case, you have no chance to remain unnoticed.

The STRANGERS brand is a combination of the incompatible: creativity and classics, passion and tranquility. We treat every detail with trepidation, we choose only high-quality fabrics and painstakingly develop the cut of the products: we do everything so that you cease to be unfamiliar to this world.

Well, are you ready to have a drink with us?
