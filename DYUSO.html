About

SS 2021

Concept clothing brand DYUSO

Founded in Chelyabinsk in July 2018

Founders: Julia Druzhinina and Oksana Semina.

If we combine the capital letters of our names, we get the brand name.

We work with premium fabrics, sew limited collections, often broadcast transformers in our capsules.

The shared dream for the two was to create a conceptual brand that would reflect where we live. And in July 2018, we launched the first capsule, which was a great success. Since then, we have been charged with the "glowing eyes" of customers who inspire us to creatively design each product.

If you look at our collections, you will see that they are daring, made, most often, in achromatic colors, but at the same time very individual and with their own history. This is all that is typical for the city of Chelyabinsk. We are very capricious, but we value individuality. Therefore, our clothes are for brave, self-confident girls who perceive clothes as a brush with which they paint their lives.

Our clothes are like a tool to express yourself!
